    Ep 7. The Wage Gap ft. Founder of 81cents Jordan Sale

    • The wage gap is an issue that exists in virtually every industry. Most of the time, people know that it exists but don’t understand why it’s important to alleviate other than “it’s the right thing to do”.

    Jordan, founder of 81cents, has taken this issue into her own hands. In this episode, we discuss the presence of the wage gap, it’s short term and long term impact on women and minorities, but we also walk through Jordan’s entrepreneurial journey.
